================================================================================
TECHNICAL APPROACH
================================================================================

METHOD: Interrupted Time Series Analysis (ITSA)

ARCHITECTURE: Two-Model Comparison
  1. Baseline Model: Learns normal behavior (no policy)
  2. Policy Model: Learns behavior with policy influence
  3. Impact Calculation: Policy - Baseline = Incremental Impact

ALGORITHM: Gradient Boosting Regressor
  - n_estimators: 100-150
  - learning_rate: 0.1
  - max_depth: 5-6
  - Handles non-linear relationships
  - Robust to outliers

VALIDATION:
  - Train/test split (80/20)
  - Cross-validation ready
  - Performance metrics: MAE, RMSE, R²
  - Target: R² > 0.80

PRIVACY:
  - No individual-level data
  - Only system-level aggregations
  - Complies with UIDAI guidelines
